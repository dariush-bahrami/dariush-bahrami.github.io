I like understanding how things work — from math on paper to code running
on hardware. This blog is my notebook.

I'm a curious person by nature. Science, engineering, programming,
artificial intelligence — I'm drawn to anything that rewards digging deeper.
I never consider myself an expert in any subject, just someone who enjoys
learning and solving problems.

Professionally I've worked across machine learning — computer vision,
natural language processing, data science. But curiosity doesn't stay
in one lane, and neither does this blog.

English is not my first language, so I occasionally polish my writing
with AI assistance — as you may have already guessed from the suspicious
number of em dashes.
